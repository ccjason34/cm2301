Bond Discount and Premium as a Seesaw

Bond pricing depends on the relationship between the stated interest rate and the market interest rate. Then discounts or premiums are amortized over the bond's life.

Step 1 of 7

Compare the stated rate with the market rate

The stated rate is printed on the bond. The market rate is what investors demand today.
