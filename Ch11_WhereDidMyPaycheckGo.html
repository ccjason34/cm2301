Where Did My Paycheck Go?

Gross pay is not the same as take-home pay. Payroll deductions create current liabilities, and employer payroll taxes are a separate employer cost.

Step 1 of 6

Start with gross pay

The employee earns $2,000 before deductions. That is gross pay, not the amount deposited in the employee's bank account.
