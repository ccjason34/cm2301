Suggested 5-Week Route

A compact path for a fast summer term.

Week 1
Chapters 1, 2, 3 Business environment, transaction recording, adjusting entries.
Week 2
Chapters 4, 5, 6 Cycle review, merchandising, inventory cost flow.
Week 3
Chapters 8, 9 Receivables, plant assets, depletion, amortization.
Week 4
Chapters 10, 11 Investments, payroll, current liabilities.
Week 5
Chapters 12, 13 Bonds, long-term liabilities, stockholders' equity.
