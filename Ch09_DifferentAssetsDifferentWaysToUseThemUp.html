Ch 9 · Depreciation, Depletion & Amortization

Different Assets, Different Ways to Use Them Up

Long-term assets help the business over more than one period. Accounting spreads the cost over the periods that receive the benefit.

Step 1 of 5

One idea, three asset types

A truck, a coal mine or timber stand, and a patent all provide benefits over time. The cost should be spread over that benefit period.
